## Description

Find the bets the market got wrong.

Value Bets delivers data-driven sports picks backed by a probability model with a verified 66.4% moneyline win rate and +28.3% ROI across five years of MLB backtesting (2021-2025). No gut calls, no touts — just math that beats the books.

WHAT YOU GET

- Daily value picks across moneyline, totals, and run line markets
- Edge percentage showing exactly how much the model disagrees with the books
- Expected value (EV) and Kelly criterion sizing for every pick
- Sharp line move and Pinnacle confirmation signals
- Live 2026 season record with win rate and ROI tracking
- 7-day settled pick history with closing line value (CLV) verification

HOW IT WORKS

The model runs every morning before first pitch. It compares its win probability estimate against the odds at major sportsbooks. When the true probability exceeds the implied probability — that's a value bet. Every pick shows the model's math: edge %, fair odds, and optimal Kelly stake.

FREE & PRO TIERS

Free accounts receive a curated selection of daily moneyline picks. Full access to every pick across all markets is available through your account at mlbvaluebets.com.

This app requires an existing mlbvaluebets.com account to sign in. Account management and subscriptions are handled on the web.

STARTING WITH MLB — MORE SPORTS COMING

Value Bets launches with MLB coverage powered by our most mature model. Additional sports will be added as models reach production-grade accuracy.
